Things to Do in Sucre

Discover the most popular attractions in Bolivia's most beautiful city

Plaza 25 de Mayo

Plaza 25 de Mayo

The heart of Sucre, this beautiful main square is surrounded by colonial architecture and is perfect for people-watching and soaking in the local atmosphere.

Casa de la Libertad

Casa de la Libertad

Where Bolivia's independence was declared in 1825. This historic building houses important documents and artifacts from Bolivia's fight for independence.

Catedral Metropolitana

Catedral Metropolitana

This stunning cathedral features beautiful colonial architecture and houses the famous "Virgen de Guadalupe" painting, one of Bolivia's most important religious artworks.

Dinosaur Tracks

Dinosaur Tracks

Visit Cal Orck'o, the world's largest dinosaur tracksite, with over 5,000 footprints from various dinosaur species preserved in a limestone wall.

Mercado Central

Mercado Central

Experience local life at the central market, where you can find fresh produce, traditional foods, and handmade crafts from local artisans.

Mirador de la Recoleta

Mirador de la Recoleta

Enjoy panoramic views of Sucre from this viewpoint, especially beautiful at sunset. The area also features the Recoleta Monastery and artisan shops.
